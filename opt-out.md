# Opt-out / Uninstall (Quiz Builder)

If you no longer want to use Quiz Builder or you want to opt out of testing, you can uninstall the add-on from Google Docs.

## Uninstall from Google Docs

1. Open the Google Doc where Quiz Builder is installed.
2. Click **Extensions** → **Add-ons** → **Manage add-ons**.
3. Find **Quiz Builder**.
4. Click **⋮ (More)** → **Remove**.
5. Confirm the removal.

After uninstalling, the add-on will no longer run in your documents.

## Opt out of testing

If you were added as a tester and want us to remove your account from the testing list, contact us with the email address you used for testing:

- Email: developer@visiprima.id

Please include:
- Your email address
- Your Google Workspace domain (if applicable)
- A short note that you want to opt out of testing
